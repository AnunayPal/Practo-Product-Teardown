# 🏥 Practo Assurance
### Redesigning Appointment Reliability to Build Patient Trust

> A Product Management Case Study focused on reducing appointment uncertainty and improving trust in healthcare bookings.

---

## 📌 Overview

Practo has simplified healthcare discovery and appointment booking for millions of users across India.

However, after analyzing hundreds of user reviews, I noticed a recurring pattern:

> Patients weren't struggling to find doctors.

They were struggling to trust whether a confirmed appointment would actually happen.

Last-minute cancellations, unavailable doctors, and poor communication created uncertainty throughout the booking journey.

This case study explores how Practo can improve appointment reliability through a trust-first product experience.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Users frequently experience:

- Last-minute appointment cancellations
- Doctors becoming unavailable after confirmation
- Lack of proactive communication
- No seamless recovery experience
- Reduced trust in online healthcare booking

**How might Practo ensure that every confirmed appointment feels reliable and trustworthy?**

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

## Practo Assurance

A reliability layer built into the booking journey.

Key Features

- ✅ Doctor Reliability Score
- ✅ Real-Time Appointment Status
- ✅ Smart Rebooking Assistant
- ✅ Trust Badges
- ✅ Instant Cancellation Recovery
- ✅ Patient Notifications
